Our Story

Crafted with Care, Served with Joy

What started as a passion for creating beautiful, alcohol-free drinks for friends has blossomed into a full catering experience. At mocktailsbyify, every drink is handcrafted using fresh, seasonal ingredients — because your guests deserve something extraordinary.

Whether it's a sun-soaked garden wedding or an intimate birthday dinner, we bring the bar to you — complete with bespoke menus, stunning presentation, and drinks that taste as good as they look.
